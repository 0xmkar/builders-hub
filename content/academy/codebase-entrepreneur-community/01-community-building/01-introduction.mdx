## What Makes Web3 Community Building Different

Traditional social media strategies often fall short in Web3 because:

1. **Trust is paramount**: In a space filled with scams and rug pulls, authentic relationships matter more than follower counts
2. **Community equals ownership**: Your community members often become token holders and stakeholders
3. **Education is essential**: Most potential users need education about both your project and Web3 in general
4. **Global reach from day one**: Web3 communities are inherently international and operate 24/7
